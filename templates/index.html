About us

Here at Recipe Hub we are dedicated to providing a platform for you, our community, to create, read, update and delete your own recipes. The goal of this community is to use a centralised location to store recipes from all corners of globe.

Features

Here are the features that you can look forward to on our site

  • Log in with your own personal user profile
  • Create, update and delete your own recipes as well as read other community members ideas
  • We have teamed up with the good people over at FatSecret™ so that you have a built in calorie counter in order to know exactly how many you are eating
